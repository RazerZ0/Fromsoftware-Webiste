Dark Souls Trilogy

The Dark Souls trilogy is a series of action RPGs set in a dying fantasy world cursed by an endless cycle of fire and darkness. Players assume the role of an undead warrior struggling against powerful enemies and fallen gods. The lore focuses on decay, sacrifice, and repetition, with stories hidden in environmental design, item descriptions, and fragmented dialogue. Across all three games, the trilogy presents a bleak universe where the player’s actions determine whether the cycle continues or ends.
