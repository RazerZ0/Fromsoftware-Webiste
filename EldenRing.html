Elden Ring Elden Ring is an open-world action RPG where players explore the vast Lands Between, a realm once governed by the power of the Elden Ring. After the Ring is shattered, the world falls into chaos, with demigods fighting for its fragments. The game combines exploration, challenging combat, and deep character customization, while its lore is told indirectly through the world, item descriptions, and NPCs. Themes of power, ambition, and the nature of order shape its rich mythological narrative.
