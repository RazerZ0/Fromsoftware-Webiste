Bloodborne

Bloodborne Bloodborne is a gothic action RPG set in the decaying city of Yharnam, a place obsessed with healing through mysterious blood rituals. As the game progresses, the narrative shifts from classic horror to cosmic terror, revealing the influence of ancient beings known as Great Ones. The gameplay encourages aggressive combat, reflecting the themes of madness and desperation present in the lore. Storytelling is intentionally cryptic, requiring players to interpret clues found in the environment and item descriptions. Bloodborne also has a DLC, named "The Old Hunters"
