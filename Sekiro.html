Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

Sekiro is an action-adventure game focused on precise sword combat and skill-based gameplay rather than traditional RPG mechanics. Players take on the role of a shinobi tasked with protecting a young lord connected to the power of immortality. Set in a fictional version of feudal Japan, the lore explores honor, loyalty, and the consequences of seeking eternal life. Unlike other FromSoftware titles, the story is more direct, with clear character motivations and narrative progression.
